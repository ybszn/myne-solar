About Myne.Solar

Founded in 2025 and based in Lagos, Nigeria, Myne.Solar is dedicated to transforming the energy landscape with clean, reliable solar solutions. Our mission is to provide affordable, sustainable energy to homes and businesses across Lagos, reducing carbon footprints and energy costs.

Our team of experts brings years of experience in solar technology, ensuring every installation meets the highest standards. We understand the unique challenges of Lagos—its sunny climate, growing population, and energy needs—and we tailor our solutions accordingly.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, reliable, and sustainable solar energy solutions to Lagos residents and businesses, powering a brighter future.
